<p><p>Energy harvesting systems capture and convert ambient energy into usable electrical power, and they can be categorized into four main types. Photovoltaic systems harness solar energy using solar cells. Thermoelectric devices generate electricity from temperature differences. Piezoelectric systems produce energy through mechanical stress, converting vibrations into electrical power. Electrodynamic systems utilize motion in magnetic fields to generate electricity. Each type serves diverse applications, contributing to the growing trend of sustainable energy solutions and reducing reliance on traditional power sources.</p></p>
