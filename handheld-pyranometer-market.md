<p><ul><li>Thermopile</li><li>Photovoltaic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld pyranometer market is segmented into thermopile and photovoltaic types. Thermopile pyranometers utilize multiple thermocouples to measure solar radiation by converting thermal energy into a voltage signal, offering high accuracy over a wide range of conditions. In contrast, photovoltaic pyranometers employ a solar cell to generate an electrical output proportional to the incident solar radiation, making them lightweight and easier to use. Both types are crucial for evaluating solar energy potential in various applications.</p></p>
