<p><p>Conductive Silicone Rubber (CSE) market is categorized by various processing methods, including compression molding, extrusion, injection molding, and others. Compression molding involves compressing heated silicone into a mold, ensuring precise shapes and sizes. Extrusion allows continuous production of profiles and custom shapes. Injection molding injects silicone into molds under high pressure for intricate designs. Other methods may include casting or thermal forming, catering to specific applications across industries such as electronics, automotive, and healthcare, where conductivity and flexibility are essential.</p></p>
