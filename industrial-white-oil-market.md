<p><p>The Industrial White Oil market is primarily divided into two types: paraffin-based and cycloalkyl. Paraffin-based white oils are derived from refining processes of petroleum, known for their high purity and low toxicity, making them suitable for applications in personal care, food, and pharmaceuticals. Conversely, cycloalkyl white oils, derived from cycloalkane structures, offer enhanced stability and performance in high-temperature industrial applications. Both types are valued for their versatility, safety, and effectiveness across various sectors, including cosmetics, textiles, and manufacturing.</p></p>
