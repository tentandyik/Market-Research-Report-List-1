<p><p>Flexible electronics and circuits can be categorized into three main market types: single-sided, multilayer, and rigid. Single-sided circuits feature components on one side of a flexible substrate, making them lightweight and cost-effective. Multilayer circuits involve multiple layers of conductive materials, allowing for more complex circuitry in compact forms. Rigid flexible electronics combine rigid and flexible elements, providing durability and versatility for various applications. Each type caters to specific needs in sectors like consumer electronics, medical devices, and automotive.</p></p>
