<p><p>The Steam & Water Analysis System (SWAS) market encompasses various types of analyses essential for optimizing boiler operations and ensuring safety. Condensate analysis monitors the purity of recovered steam condensate, while boiler feed water analysis ensures the quality of water entering the boiler. Boiler water analysis focuses on the chemical composition and health of water within the boiler, and saturated steam analysis evaluates steam quality and efficiency. Together, these analyses help maintain efficient operations and prevent equipment damage.</p></p>
