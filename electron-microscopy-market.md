<p><strong>The Electron Microscopy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Electron Microscope</li><li>Scanning Electron Microscope</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electron microscopy market can be segmented into three main types: Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM), Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM), and Others. TEM offers high-resolution imaging by transmitting electrons through thin samples, making it ideal for detailed structural analysis at the atomic level. SEM focuses on surface morphology by scanning the specimen's surface with a focused electron beam, providing 3D images. The Others category includes advanced techniques like scanning transmission electron microscopy (STEM) and electron energy loss spectroscopy (EELS), catering to specialized research needs.</p></p>
