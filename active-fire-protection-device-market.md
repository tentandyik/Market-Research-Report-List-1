<p><p>The Active Fire Protection Device market includes various types of fire suppression systems. Foam systems create a barrier between the fuel and oxygen, ideal for liquid fires. Carbon dioxide systems displace oxygen, quickly extinguishing flames without leaving residues, suitable for electronic equipment. Other systems may incorporate water mist, wet chemical agents, or specialized solutions tailored for specific fire risks.</p></p>
