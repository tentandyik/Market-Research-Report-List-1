<p><p>Four-way reversing markets control fluid flow in various applications. The pilot-type utilizes a small pilot valve to trigger the main valve, allowing for precise control. The slide-type features a sliding mechanism that shifts positions to regulate flow direction, offering smooth transitions. The poppet-type uses a spring-loaded poppet valve, providing a reliable seal and efficient operation. Each type effectively enables the switching of pathways in hydraulic or pneumatic systems, ensuring optimal management of pressure and flow rates across different scenarios.</p></p>
