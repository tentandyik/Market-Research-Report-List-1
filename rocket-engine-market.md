<p><p>The rocket engine market is primarily divided into two main types: liquid rocket engines and solid rocket engines. Liquid rocket engines utilize propellant in a liquid state, offering controllable thrust and the ability to restart, making them ideal for precision missions. In contrast, solid rocket engines use a solid propellant that provides high thrust and simplicity in design but lack restart capability. Both types cater to various applications, including space exploration, satellite launches, and defense, each with distinct advantages and limitations.</p></p>
