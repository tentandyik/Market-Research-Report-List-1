<p><p>A welding diode is a semiconductor device that plays a crucial role in electrical circuits used in welding applications. These diodes are essential components in various welding machines and systems, including arc, MIG, and TIG welding, due to their high efficiency and reliability.</p><p>The Welding Diode Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 3.80% during the forecast period.
