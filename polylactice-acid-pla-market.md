The PLA market comprises three main types: Poly(L-lactic) acid, known for its high thermal stability and mechanical strength; Poly(D-lactic) acid, which exhibits lower melting points and is suitable for specific applications; and Poly(DL-lactic) acid, a racemic mixture that offers a balance of properties. Each type serves distinct purposes, enhancing the versatility and appeal of PLA in various industries.</p></p>
