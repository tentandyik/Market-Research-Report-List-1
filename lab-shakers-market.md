<p><ul><li>Room Temperature Type</li><li>Constant or Low Temperature</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lab shakers are categorized into two primary market types based on temperature control: Room Temperature and Constant or Low Temperature. Room Temperature shakers operate at ambient conditions, suitable for general mixing and culturing applications. In contrast, Constant or Low Temperature shakers maintain specific, lower temperatures, ideal for sensitive biological processes and sample preservation, such as enzyme reactions or cell cultures that require strict thermal control. These distinctions cater to varying experimental requirements in laboratory settings.</p></p>
