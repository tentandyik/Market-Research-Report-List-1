<p><strong>The Open Peripheral Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic Peripheral Pump</li><li>Centrifugal Peripheral Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The open peripheral pump market comprises various types including magnetic peripheral pumps and centrifugal peripheral pumps. Magnetic peripheral pumps utilize magnetic coupling to achieve efficient fluid movement without direct contact with the motor, enhancing reliability and maintenance-free operation. In contrast, centrifugal peripheral pumps employ a rotating impeller to generate flow, characterized by high efficiency and versatility for various applications. Both types cater to different industrial needs, offering distinct advantages in fluid transfer depending on the operational requirements.</p></p>
