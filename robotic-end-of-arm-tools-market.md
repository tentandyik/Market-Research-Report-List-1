<p><p>The robotic end-of-arm tools market can be categorized into four main types: electrical, hydraulical, mechanical, and pneumatical. </p><p>Electrical tools utilize motors for precision and control, ideal for tasks requiring intricate manipulation. Hydraulical tools leverage fluid pressure for high force applications, suitable for heavy lifting and robust operations. Mechanical tools rely on gears and linkages for simple yet effective movement. Pneumatical tools use compressed air for quick, light operations, making them efficient for repetitive tasks and dynamic environments. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
