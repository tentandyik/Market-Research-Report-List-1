Paddle mixers use flat blades to efficiently blend thicker materials, making them suitable for viscous substances. Turbine mixers utilize high-speed rotating blades for rapid mixing, ideal for liquids and light solids. Magnetic mixers employ magnetic fields to agitate fluids gently without direct contact, making them useful for sterile applications.
