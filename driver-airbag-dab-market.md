<p><p>The Driver Airbag (DAB) market includes several types, primarily focusing on Frontal Airbags and Side Airbags. Frontal Airbags deploy from the steering wheel during a head-on collision, providing cushioning to reduce impact on the driver. In contrast, Side Airbags are designed to protect occupants in side-impact collisions, deploying from the side of the vehicle or seats. Both types enhance passenger safety by minimizing injuries and are integral components in modern vehicle safety systems, responding to specific crash scenarios.</p></p>
