SSRIs primarily target serotonin reuptake, providing relief from depression commonly associated with neurodegenerative disorders. Dopamine inhibitors are essential in treating disorders like Parkinson's, as they modulate dopamine levels to alleviate motor symptoms. Together, these treatments address diverse symptoms and progression of neurodegenerative diseases, enhancing patient care.</p></p>
