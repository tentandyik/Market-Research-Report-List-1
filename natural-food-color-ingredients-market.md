<p><p>The natural food color ingredients market includes several key types. Carotenoids, derived from plants, provide yellow to red hues and are used in a variety of foods. Anthocyanins offer vibrant red, purple, and blue colors, primarily found in fruits. Chlorophyll imparts green shades, mainly sourced from green plants. Spirulina extract, a blue-green algae, adds color and nutritional benefits. Annatto gives an orange tint, while curcumin, from turmeric, offers yellow; paprika adds red-orange tones, enhancing both aesthetic and flavor in food products.</p></p>
