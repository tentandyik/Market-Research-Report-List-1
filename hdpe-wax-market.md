<p><strong>The HDPE Wax Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidation Type HDPE Wax</li><li>Non-oxidation Type HDPE Wax</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE wax market is categorized into two main types: oxidation type and non-oxidation type. Oxidation type HDPE wax is produced through the controlled oxidation of high-density polyethylene, resulting in a wax with enhanced polarity and improved adhesion properties, making it suitable for applications in inks, coatings, and adhesives. Non-oxidation type HDPE wax, on the other hand, retains the properties of unmodified HDPE, offering excellent lubrication and anti-blocking characteristics, commonly used in plastics and packaging industries.</p></p>
