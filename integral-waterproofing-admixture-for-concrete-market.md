<p><p>Integral waterproofing admixtures for concrete enhance the durability and water resistance of structures. The crystalline type forms crystalline structures within the concrete that block water pathways and react with moisture, effectively sealing the material. Pore-blocking types contain substances that fill the pores in the concrete matrix, preventing water ingress. Other types may include various additives, such as hydrophobic agents or surface treatments, aimed at improving water resistance and enhancing the longevity of the concrete without altering its fundamental properties.</p></p>
