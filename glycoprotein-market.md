<p><strong>The Glycoprotein Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>N-linkage</li><li>O-linkage</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glycoprotein market is categorized mainly into three types based on glycan attachment: N-linkage, O-linkage, and others. N-linked glycoproteins have carbohydrates attached to the nitrogen atom of asparagine, playing vital roles in protein stability and cell signaling. O-linked glycoproteins feature sugar molecules linked to the oxygen atom of serine or threonine, which influence cellular communication and immune responses. The 'others' category includes less common glycosylation types, expanding the potential applications in therapeutics, diagnostics, and biotechnology.</p></p>
