<p><strong>The ATV Air Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Foam-based Filters</li><li>Paper-based Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ATV air filter market consists primarily of two types: foam-based filters and paper-based filters. Foam-based filters are designed for high filtration efficiency and durability, making them ideal for off-road conditions. They are often washable and reusable, offering long-term cost savings. In contrast, paper-based filters provide excellent filtration for standard conditions, capturing fine particles effectively. They are generally more affordable but less durable than foam filters. Each type suits different riding scenarios and maintenance preferences.</p></p>
