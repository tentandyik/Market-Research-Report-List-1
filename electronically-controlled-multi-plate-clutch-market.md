<p><p>The electronically controlled multi-plate clutch market is primarily segmented into two types: one-way clutches and two-way clutches. One-way clutches engage in a single direction, allowing torque transfer while preventing backslip, making them ideal for applications needing precise control, like automotive gear systems. Conversely, two-way clutches can engage and disengage in both directions, offering enhanced flexibility for applications requiring bidirectional torque transmission. This differentiation caters to diverse operational needs across industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery.</p></p>
