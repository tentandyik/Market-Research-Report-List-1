<p><p>Quicklime, slaked lime, and hydraulic lime are essential forms of calcium compounds used extensively in various industries, including construction, metallurgy, and environmental applications. Quicklime, or calcium oxide, is produced by heating limestone and is utilized in steel production, water treatment, and as a soil stabilizer. Slaked lime, or calcium hydroxide, is derived from quicklime by adding water and is commonly used in construction, especially for mortar and plaster. Hydraulic lime, which sets through hydration in wet conditions, is favored in construction for its durability and reduced environmental impact.</p><p>The Quicklime, Slaked Lime, and Hydraulic Lime Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand in the construction sector, particularly in emerging economies.
