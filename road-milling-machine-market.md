<p><p>The road milling machine market is categorized primarily into wheeled and tracked types. Wheeled road milling machines are designed for enhanced mobility and swift transport across paved surfaces, making them suitable for smaller projects and urban applications. In contrast, tracked road milling machines offer superior stability and traction, making them ideal for rugged terrains and larger-scale operations, such as highway rehabilitation and deeper milling tasks.
