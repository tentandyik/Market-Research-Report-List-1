<p><p>Air freight services can be categorized into two main types: On-Board Courier (OBC) and Daily Flight markets. OBC involves a dedicated courier accompanying the cargo on a commercial flight, ensuring swift and secure delivery, often used for urgent or sensitive shipments. In contrast, the Daily Flight market utilizes scheduled airline services to transport cargo, catering to regular shipments, which may require more time but provides cost-effective solutions for larger volumes. Both types play vital roles in global logistics.</p></p>
