<p><p>The Calcium Hypochlorite Technology Market consists of two primary types: the Calcium Process and the Sodium Process. The Calcium Process involves the reaction of calcium hydroxide with chlorine, producing calcium hypochlorite, commonly used for water treatment and sanitation. In contrast, the Sodium Process combines sodium hydroxide with chlorine, resulting in sodium hypochlorite, primarily employed in bleaching and disinfecting applications. Both processes cater to different market needs, influencing their demand and application across various industries, notably water treatment and cleaning.</p></p>
